ML Intermediate -Classification Playground

🎯 Objective

Welcome to the ultimate playground of data classification! Your mission, should you choose to accept it, is to delve into this dataset and predict income levels based on various intriguing features. Team up with your fellow data wizards to create powerful classification models and showcase your skills!

🛠️ Helpful Steps

  1. 🧐 Get to Know Your Dataset:

    • Dive into the data dictionary and become best friends with each feature.
    • Identify your target variable (the big prize: income!).

  2. 🔧 Data Preprocessing Party:

    • Wave goodbye to any missing values!
    • Transform categorical variables into numerical superheroes (one-hot encoding, here we come!).
    • Normalize or standardize numerical features to keep things in check.

  3. 📈 Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) Adventure:

    • Unleash your inner data artist with visualizations (histograms, box plots, oh my!).
    • Discover the hidden relationships and trends in the data.

  4. 🤖 Model Selection Madness:

    • Choose your weapons! Will it be Logistic Regression, Decision Trees, or perhaps a Random Forest?
    • Split the data into a training set (where models learn) and a testing set (where they prove their worth).

  5. 💪 Model Training Bootcamp:

    • Train your models like a pro on the training set.
    • Tweak those hyperparameters to achieve peak performance with Grid Search or Random Search.

  6. 🏆 Model Evaluation Showdown:

    • Evaluate your models using accuracy, precision, recall, and F1-score. Who’s the champion?
    • Visualize the results with confusion matrices for an epic reveal!

  7. 🔍 Final Analysis Fiesta:

    • Discover which features are the stars of your predictions.
    • Draw some insightful conclusions and share the treasure you’ve uncovered!

  8. 📜 Document Your Journey:

    • Keep a record of your process, findings, and any hilarious (or frustrating) moments you encountered.

🎊 Conclusion

By the end of this adventure, you’ll not only master the art of data classification but also have a blast working with your teammates! Let the fun begin, and may the best data scientist win!
